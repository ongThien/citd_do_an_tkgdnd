Couples' Retreat in Vietnam

Explore Vietnam's most beautiful spots with your special someone in this itinerary for two.
4.8/5

(777 reviewes)

Duration

6 days

Tour type

Couples

Group size

2

Location

Ha Long Bay

Overview

  • Ha Long Bay:
    Few experiences are more romantic than seeing sunset on a ship in Halong, or waking up surrounded by magnificent karsts. Tour fishing villages, go kayaking, or simply sail away.

  • Hoi An:
    Hoi An's flowering gardens and rice paddy fields are ideal for nature lovers. Book a hotel along the river or by the beach to be near the water, and stroll hand-in-hand through the Ancient Town.

  • Phu Quoc:
    This island paradise can be as luxurious or laidback as you make it. Take an island-hopping tour to soak up the sun, and unwind in style at Phu Quoc's sleek rooftop bars and beach clubs.

Itinerary

Day 1

Head to Ha Long Bay for a magical cruise.
Day 2

Wake up amid Ha Long's incredible seascapes, enjoy a leisurely breakfast and cruise back to the harbour, return to Hanoi and fly to Hoi An.
Day 3

Do a cooking or cycling tour, see the Ancient Town
Day 4

Spend a morning on An Bang beach, fly to Phu Quoc.
Day 5

Go island-hopping, swimming and snorkeling with a day tour in the An Thoi Archipelago, then visit the night market to dine on seafood specialties.
Day 6

Have a pool day and check out the beach bars.
